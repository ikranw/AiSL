## Self-Assessment

### Part A

I worked on building our 3D avatar, Linda, and enabling her to sign in American Sign Language while connecting that functionality to our backend translating pipeline.
To do this, I used Unity, C#, and other tools to implement avatar movement and animation, and to ensure that the output from our translation system could be 
represented visually. This required me to debug issues with animation, handle integration challenges between different components, and continuously test how 
the system behaved as a whole. While there were obstacles with integration, we were able to overcome them by using WebGL and increasing storage capacity, 
which ultimately allowed us to publish our application live. I also coordinated with the ASL community on campus to get user feedback.

I was able to build upon the skills I identified in my initial assessment, especially in programming and learning new technologies. I improved my 
proficiency in C# and gained hands-on experience working in Unity, which was completely new to me before this project. I also learned a lot about 
resolving merge conflicts, integrating multiple applications into one cohesive system, and writing scripts for animation. One of my main successes was 
contributing to a working avatar system connected to our pipeline, while a key challenge was managing unexpected technical issues during integration. 
Overall, I developed stronger problem-solving skills, adaptability, and a deeper understanding of full-stack system development.

### Part B

My group accomplished a lot throughout this project, including developing a functioning website and a working prototype for our final demo. We were 
able to bring together different components into a cohesive system and demonstrate the core idea behind our project. As a team, we also gained significant 
experience using GitHub and Git, especially when it came to resolving merge conflicts and collaborating on shared code. We communicated consistently,

Our teamwork was very effective overall, as we maintained a balanced workload and supported each other whenever challenges came up. Everyone contributed meaningfully,

solutions. This experience showed me how important collaboration, communication, and mutual support are in successfully completing a complex project.
