# User Guide

## What is AiSL?
AiSL is an ASL learning application that translates English sentences into proper ASL grammar and displays them through a 3D avatar.

---

## How to Use AiSL

1. Open the app   
2. Type an English sentence  
3. Watch the avatar sign the sentence  
4. Replay or slow down the signing to practice  

---

## Tips
- Use full sentences  
- Keep sentences simple  
- Replay signs often  

---

## Important Notes
- AiSL does not translate sentences literally word-for-word
